Why SpeakTwo

An interpreter that sits on the table

Built for real, in-person conversations — fast, private, and effortless.

Live two-way translation

Both directions stream simultaneously. The model auto-detects which language is being spoken from the audio — no manual switching.

Two display modes

Face-to-face splits the screen with the top panel rotated 180° for the person across from you. Side-by-side shows one chronological chat.

Tuned for the room

Microphone-scenario presets for phone-in-hand vs. on-the-table, plus optional auto-leveling to balance near and far speakers.

Local archive

Past sessions are saved on-device so you can scroll back through full transcripts and translations whenever you need them.

Privacy by design

Your API key lives in the iOS Keychain. Audio goes straight to OpenAI — nothing is routed through a developer-operated backend.

Built-in diagnostics

A live log of the realtime connection makes debugging transparent, with automatic reconnection on transient socket failures.

How it works

From silence to conversation in three taps

Add your key & pick languages

Paste your OpenAI API key once — it’s stored in the Keychain — then choose your language and the other person’s.

Put the phone down

Set it on the table between you and tap Start. SpeakTwo opens two realtime sessions, one per direction.

Just talk, naturally

Speak in your own language. Each side is transcribed and translated live and shown to the right person, the right way up.

Face-to-face mode

Designed for the person across the table

  • Rotated panel — the top half flips 180° so the other person reads it naturally without grabbing the phone.
  • Color-coded sides — each speaker’s language has its own panel, so it’s always clear who said what.
  • Hands-free — no push-to-talk. The model figures out the source language from the audio.
Side-by-side chat

A clean transcript you can follow and revisit

  • One chronological thread — every utterance shows what was said and its translation, in order.
  • Saved on-device — open the archive later to review the whole conversation.
  • Switch anytime — toggle between face-to-face and chat without losing the session.

Privacy & cost

Your key. Your audio. No middleman.

SpeakTwo is Bring-Your-Own-Key with no subscription and no backend. You pay OpenAI directly for realtime usage, and the app tracks it locally so there are no surprises.

  • API key stored only in the iOS Keychain on your device.
  • Audio streams directly to OpenAI over WebSocket — nothing through a third-party server.
  • Transcripts saved only on-device. No accounts, no analytics backend.
